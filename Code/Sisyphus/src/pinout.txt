CNC pins on Arduino:

13 = Spindle Direction
12 = Spindle Enable
11 = Limit Z
10 = Limit Y
9 = Limit X
8 = Stepper Enable
7 = Direction Z
6 = Direction Y
5 = Direction X
4 = Step Z
3 = Step Y
2 = Step X

A3 = Coolant Enable
A2 = Cycle Start
A1 = Feed Hold
A0 = Reset/Abort

Bluetooth module pins:

TX3
RX3

3.5 V
GND

50 = MISO
51 = MOSI
52 = SCK
53 = CS
